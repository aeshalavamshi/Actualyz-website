# Actualyz — Personalised Mentorship Made Accessible

Actualyz is an education mentorship platform connecting students across India with qualified mentors who have cleared top competitive exams — CAT, UPSC, IIT JEE, NEET, and more.

## What We Offer

- **Career Discovery** — Psychometric assessment for students below Class 10 to identify natural strengths and ideal career paths
- **Exam Strategy** — Personalised one-on-one mentorship for JEE, NEET, UPSC, CAT, and other competitive exams
