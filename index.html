Our Flagship Assessment

The Actualyz
Psychometric Test.

The same science Fortune 500 companies use to identify future leaders — now applied to help your child find their right path before they choose a stream. Click any dimension to learn what we measure and why it matters.

💡

Personality Type

+

Uncover the cognitive style your child is naturally wired with.

We map your child's cognitive style using an MBTI-based framework across four dimensions: how they process the world (Introvert/Extrovert), what they pay attention to (Intuitive/Sensing), how they make decisions (Thinking/Feeling), and how they organise their life (Judging/Perceiving). This reveals which career environments they are naturally wired to thrive in — before they commit to one.
🤝

Social & Leadership Skills

+

Assess interpersonal strengths, communication and leadership potential.

Measures interpersonal communication strength, comfort with authority and hierarchy, collaborative versus competitive drive, and natural leadership style — whether your child leads by influence, expertise, or formal authority. Shows which professional environments will energise them and which will drain them over a career.
🧮

Maths & Logical Reasoning

+

Measure analytical thinking and quantitative aptitude objectively.

Objectively scores numerical ability, logical reasoning, pattern recognition, and spatial visualisation through timed tasks — not self-reported questionnaires. Directly predicts suitability for JEE, data-heavy careers, and quantitative fields. The score reflects genuine aptitude and cannot be inflated by coaching or short-term preparation.
📖

Language & Expression

+

Evaluate verbal ability, comprehension and clarity of communication.

Measures verbal ability, reading comprehension depth, and the preference for written versus spoken communication. Identifies whether your child is better at processing or producing language — a distinction that changes which careers feel natural versus exhausting over an entire career. Critical input for recommending law, civil services, journalism, or management paths.
🔬

Scientific Aptitude

+

Gauge genuine interest and ability across scientific domains.

Goes beyond 'does my child like science' to measure actual mechanical and scientific reasoning ability. Gauges genuine interest and aptitude across physics, chemistry, and biology — distinguishing students who enjoy the subject from those who are genuinely built to succeed professionally in a science-based career. Prevents the most common and expensive mistake: pushing a child into NEET or JEE preparation without this clarity.
👁️

Learning Style

+

Visual, auditory or kinesthetic — know how your child learns best.

Identifies whether your child learns best through visuals, listening, physical experience, or reading and writing — using the VARK model. Most students study using methods that do not match how their brain actually processes information. Correcting this single mismatch can dramatically reduce study hours and improve retention — without changing the content they study at all.
🌟

Motivational Wiring

+

Discover what truly drives your child — independent of external pressure.

Uses situational scenarios — not direct questions — to surface intrinsic versus extrinsic motivation. Identifies your child's core career drivers from a structured set: adventure and risk-taking, creativity, social service, continuous learning, independence, structured environments, or high-paced competitive settings. The distinction between intrinsic and extrinsic motivation is the single strongest predictor of long-term career satisfaction.
👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Aspirations Test

+

A separate test for parents to surface hopes and align them with strengths.

Parents take a separate assessment alongside the student. This generates the Parent-Child Alignment Score — the emotional centrepiece of the discussion session. It maps precisely where your aspirations and your child's natural profile converge, and where they diverge. No other assessment in India offers this. The gap between these two profiles — not the profiles themselves — is what the mentor discussion resolves.
🗺️

Career Shortlisting

+

From hundreds of careers, we identify the best-fit options for your family discussion.

From hundreds of career options, we shortlist the 3–5 paths that best fit your child's complete profile across all dimensions above. Each path is presented with the required exam, preparation timeline, and — uniquely — an AI exposure rating showing how vulnerable that specific role is to automation over the next 15 years. You don't just know what to pursue. You know whether it will still matter when your child enters the workforce.
Our Differentiator — Absent from every competitor
🔥

Resilience & Grit Index

+

The single most important predictor of success in Indian competitive exams — and the one dimension no other assessment in India measures.

Every competitor measures who your child is. Only Actualyz measures what they are built to endure. This dimension captures: Grit & Perseverance (sustained effort under slow feedback loops), Pressure Response Profile (performance when time-constrained — measured via live timed task, not questionnaire), Delayed Gratification Score (measured through an interactive game that cannot be coached), and Risk Calibration (measured via a bet game across six rounds). A child can have perfect aptitude scores and fail every competitive exam because this dimension was never addressed. We surface it — and then mentor around it.

Questions

Frequently asked
questions.

How is Actualyz different from a coaching centre?+
Coaching centres run large batches with the same content for everyone. At Actualyz, every session is 1-on-1. Your mentor builds a plan around your specific test scores, weak areas and available time — not a generic syllabus shared with 200 other students.
Who are the mentors?+
Our mentors have personally cleared India's hardest exams — a civil servant who cleared UPSC Civil Services in 2016 after graduating from IIT Bombay, and an IIT Roorkee graduate who taught at Toppers Academy and co-founded a software startup. They don't just know the syllabus; they know what it takes to actually clear the exam at the highest level.
My child is in Class 8. Which stream is right for them?+
The Career Discovery stream is designed exactly for this. Students below Class 10 haven't chosen a stream yet — and this is the ideal time to do the psychometric test, understand your child's natural strengths, and make an informed choice rather than defaulting to what everyone else is doing.
How long is a session, and how often do we meet?+
Each session is approximately 45–60 minutes over a video call. Frequency is agreed with your mentor based on your goal and timeline — some students meet weekly, others fortnightly. There is no rigid package forced on you.
What happens after the first 3 introductory sessions?+
After 3 sessions, if you want to continue, sessions move to ₹500 each. There's no compulsion to commit upfront — you can pause, continue, or switch mentors. We keep it flexible because every student's journey is different.
Is it available anywhere in India?+
Yes — 100% online. All sessions happen over video call, so it doesn't matter if you're in a metro or a small town. As long as you have a stable internet connection, you can access the same quality of mentorship as anyone else.
