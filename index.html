Questions

Frequently asked
questions.

How is Actualyz different from a coaching centre?+
Coaching centres run large batches with the same content for everyone. At Actualyz, every session is 1-on-1. Your mentor builds a plan around your specific test scores, weak areas and available time — not a generic syllabus shared with 200 other students.
Who are the mentors?+
Our mentors have personally cleared India's hardest exams — a Civil Servant (CSE 2016), an IIT Roorkee graduate, and a former IRS Officer. They don't just know the syllabus; they know what it takes to actually clear the exam at the highest level.
My child is in Class 8. Which stream is right for them?+
The Career Discovery stream is designed exactly for this. Students below Class 10 haven't chosen a stream yet — and this is the ideal time to do the psychometric test, understand your child's natural strengths, and make an informed choice rather than defaulting to what everyone else is doing.
How long is a session, and how often do we meet?+
Each session is approximately 45–60 minutes over a video call. Frequency is agreed with your mentor based on your goal and timeline — some students meet weekly, others fortnightly. There is no rigid package forced on you.
What happens after the first 3 introductory sessions?+
After 3 sessions, if you want to continue, sessions move to ₹500 each. There's no compulsion to commit upfront — you can pause, continue, or switch mentors. We keep it flexible because every student's journey is different.
Is it available anywhere in India?+
Yes — 100% online. All sessions happen over video call, so it doesn't matter if you're in a metro or a small town. As long as you have a stable internet connection, you can access the same quality of mentorship as anyone else.
